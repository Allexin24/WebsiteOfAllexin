Hello! I’m Zaw Naing Winn, also known as Allexin. I’m an innovative businessman with a strong passion for creating meaningful opportunities and driving positive change. Born on April 24, 2001, my journey is fueled by a desire to make a significant impact in Myanmar’s business landscape.

My dedication to business development and growth motivates me to continually seek new avenues for success and contribute to Myanmar’s economic advancement.

My academic background includes studying Computing at Youth International University, and I am currently pursuing Geography at East Yangon University. Additionally, I hold a diploma in Other Business disciplines, which equips me with a broad range of skills and knowledge.

On a personal note, I am happily married to Su Myat Noe Oo. Outside of my professional life, I enjoy swimming, traveling, and playing futsal—activities that keep me invigorated and balanced. I also take pride in my skills in Public Relations, which enable me to build strong and lasting connections.

Green is my favorite color, symbolizing growth, vitality, and my commitment to a bright future. Looking ahead, I am excited about the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead as I continue to grow as a successful businessman and contribute positively to Myanmar’s development.
